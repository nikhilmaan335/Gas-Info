Welcome to True LPG , your guide to ensuring safety in handling LPG cylinders and preventing catastrophic accidents caused by expired cylinders. LPG (Liquefied Petroleum Gas) is a widely used energy source for cooking, heating, and various industrial applications. However, the mishandling of LPG cylinders, especially expired ones, can lead to devastating explosions and risks to life and property.
